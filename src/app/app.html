üìö Glossary of Terms

Daily Cost

The average amount you spend per day to own and operate the vehicle. Includes fuel, maintenance, and depreciation costs.

Monthly Cost

Daily cost multiplied by 30. Represents your average monthly expense for owning the vehicle.

Yearly Cost

Daily cost multiplied by 365. The annual total cost of ownership including all operational expenses.

Cost/KM

Daily cost divided by daily kilometers. Represents the cost incurred for every kilometer driven.

Fuel Cost (Daily)

Daily fuel expense calculated as: (Daily KM √∑ Fuel Efficiency) √ó Fuel Cost per unit.

Maintenance Cost (Daily)

Daily maintenance expense calculated as: Daily KM √ó Maintenance Cost per KM. Includes servicing, spare parts, and repairs.

Depreciation (Daily)

The daily loss in vehicle value. Spread over 5 years with assumption of 20% residual value after ownership.

Fuel Efficiency

Distance traveled per unit of fuel (km/liter for petrol/diesel/CNG, km/kWh for electric). Higher efficiency = lower fuel costs.

Purchase Price

The on-road cost of the vehicle (including taxes, registration, and insurance). Used to calculate depreciation.

Total Cost (ROI Years)

Cumulative cost of ownership for a specific year: Purchase Price + Operating Costs - Residual Value.

Cost Per Year

Total cost divided by number of years owned. Helps identify the optimal ownership period for best value.

Residual Value

Expected resale value of the vehicle after a given period. Depreciates 15% in year 1, then 10% annually thereafter.

Daily Kilometers

Average distance you drive per day. Used to calculate fuel consumption, maintenance, and depreciation costs.

Fuel Type

The energy source for the vehicle: Petrol, Diesel, CNG, Hybrid, or Electric. Different types have different efficiency and maintenance costs.

Most Economical

The vehicle with the lowest daily cost among all cars added to the calculator. Helps you identify the best value option.
